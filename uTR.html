UTR Match-Play

UTR (Universal Tennis Rating) Match-Play is for players of any age and level looking to play in competitive matches to develop and build their UTR rating.

We set up the date, time, and opponent of your level for you to play against.

After the match is played we will record the score into the UTR website.

If the player is in need of having a UTR profile set up, we can set that up for them.

Matches can be played singles or doubles. Scoring system to be determined.

For more information or any questions regarding UTR Match-Play please contact us.
