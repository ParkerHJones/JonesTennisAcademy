Coaches

Barbara Jones

Director and Founder

Barbara has been coaching tennis for 25+ years and continues to help those achieve their goals. She has helped people make high school teams, improve in their USTA Leagues, and even has had students play at the NCAA Division 1 level. A former Arizona State University standout Barbara was consistently playing at the highest level in college tennis and having wins over WTA professionals at that time.

Barb’s passion is passing on the skill and joy of tennis to all levels and ages. It’s her top priority that players learn good fundamentals, technique, and strategy so they can enjoy and succeed at the game of tennis throughout their life. Barb’s pro and college tennis playing experience as well as 30 + years of professional coaching make it possible for her to provide top level coaching to all levels of players from beginning to advanced.

Parker Jones

Professional

Parker brings over 20 years of experience playing tennis and 9 years of coaching. Parker played college tennis at Campbellsville University and Metropolitan State University of Denver. At Campbellsville University he was named 2nd team all american in the 2014-2015 season and that season won the NCCAA Championship in Mobile, Alabama.

“Tennis has been a pathway for all things positive in my life. I love giving back to this sport and sharing it with others.”
