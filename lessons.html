Lessons & Pricing

Group lessons start at $15/player and have 4-6 players per group.

Small group lessons consist of 3 players and are $20/player.

Semi-Private lessons consist of 2 players and are $30/player.

Private lessons are done with one player and are $60/player.

Lessons are typically 1 hour long. Half hour and hour and a half lessons are also available.

*Racket stringing available as well, please contact us for more details.*
